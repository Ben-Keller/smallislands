    Small Island Development Pillars

    UNDP’s SIDS offer – Rising Up for SIDS – presents an integrated approach for tapping into areas with potential to accelerate green recovery and transform societies based on three interconnected pillars: Climate Action, Blue Economy and Digital Transformation.
